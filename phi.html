Φάραξ

V\IV BC

  • Poralla 717; Diodorus XII 79.6-7a, XIV 63.4 (Φαρακίδας)b, 70, 79.4-6c; Pausanias VI 3.15d; Xenophon Hellenica III 2.12e, IV 5.6f; Hellenica Oxyrhynchus 7.1g; Athenaeus XII 536b; IG XI 2. 161 B 87
  • A Spartiate who took part in the battle of Mantinea in 418 as an adviser of king Agis IIa. He also participated in the battle at Aegospotamid. As nauarch of the year 398/7 he was occupied in the theater of war in Asia Minorceg. In 396/5 he was with Dionysius of Syracuse and supported him in his war against Carthageb. In summer 390 he found himself at the Isthmus with Agesilaosf.
  • Cavaignac: the generations are—Pharax 717 (the grandfather, adviser to Agis), Styphon 675 (the son), Pharax 718 (the grandson, nauarch[?], ambassador)
