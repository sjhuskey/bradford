Ὕβρίστας

III\II BC

  • Livy XXXVII 13.12 (Hybristas)
  • A Lacedaemonian pirate based on the island of Cephallenia, who closed the Ionian Sea to Italian traffic in 190 BC

Ὕγίεια

II AD

  • IG V 1. 726
  • Sister of Alexanor (1)
  • She and her brother died before they reached adulthood; their mother died with them; their father survived them.
