Ζευξίδαμος (1)

VII BC

  • Poralla 344; Pausanias III 7.6
  • Son of Archidamus
  • Father of Anaxidamos
  • Eurypontid king (in the list of Pausanias)
  • He is supposed to have succeeded his grandfather Theopompos.

Ζευξίδαμος (2)

VI\V BC

  • Poralla 345; Herodotus VI 71; Pausanias III 7.10
  • Son of Leotychidas II and his first wife
  • Collateral relative of the Eurypontid house
  • Father of Archidamus II
  • He had the second name Kyniskos.
  • He died before his father went into exile in 476.
